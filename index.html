Leaders
Of Emerging
Technologies

Emerging Technologies have the power to reshape the industries, drive economic growth and enhance our quality of life. As new innovation continues to emerge, It is crucial to celebrate the organizations and indiiduals who embrace these technologies.

Meet The Leaders

Elon Musk

Known for his work with Tesla, SpaceX, and other ventures, Musk, originally from South Africa, is a global technology visionary

Iyinoluwa Aboyeji

Co-founder of Andela and Flutterwave, Iyinoluwa has been at the forefront of building technology ecosystems in Africa.

Meet The Leaders

Amina Mohammed

As the Deputy Secretary-General of the United Nations, Amina Mohammed has been instrumental in using technology to address global challenges.

Rapelang Rabana

An entrepreneur and technology innovator, Rabana co-founded Rekindle Learning, which leverages technology for personalized education.

Meet The Leaders

Strive Masiyiwa

Founder of Econet Group, Strive Masiyiwa has played a key role in bringing telecommunications and technology to Africa.

Jack Ma

The founder of Alibaba Group who has also shown a strong commitment to fostering technology entrepreneurship and e-commerce in Africa through initiatives like the "Netpreneur Prize."

Meet The Leaders

Tim Cook

As the CEO of Apple, Tim Cook continues to lead one of the world's most influential technology companies, with a global impact on consumer technology and innovation.

Satya Nadella

As the CEO of Microsoft, Satya Nadella has overseen a period of significant growth and transformation for the company, emphasizing cloud computing and more.

Meet The Leaders

David Mba

CTO with Strategic Business Techspace is known for Capacity building of Emerging Technology Drivers in Africa on a Large Scale. He is the founder of amanda-ai.space,the first Conversational Commerce platform in Africa.

Ibrahim Maham

Ibrahim is the founder of Engineers Without Borders Ghana and co-founder of Savannah Energy, a renewable energy company that focuses on providing electricity to rural communities in Africa.

Meet The Leaders

Nunu Ntshingila

As the Head of Facebook Africa, Nunu Ntshingila is responsible for driving the social media giant's growth and engagement on the continent, connecting millions of Africans online

Erik Hersman

Erik co-founded Ushahidi, a platform for crowdsourced crisis mapping. He is also a co-founder of BRCK, a company that provides rugged internet connectivity solutions for remote and underserved areas.

Meet The Leaders

Iyore Olaye

Iyore is the founder of A.L.L. Student Loan, a fintech startup that provides affordable loans to African students. Her work addresses the financial challenges faced by students in pursuing higher education.

Chinenye Udeh

Chinenye is a computer scientist and AI researcher who has been recognized for her contributions to the development of machine learning algorithms and applications in healthcare and finance.
